Our story - Asab foundation

Founded by Brian and the Sarahs, Asab Foundation is a registerd non-profit organization dedicated to community development and social impact.

The name "Asab" signifies the foundation's mission to support and uplift communities in need.

"Asab Foundation strives to foster positive change by providing resources, support, and opportunities for community growth, education, and empowerment."

Kizito Brian

WHAT WE DO

Our Involvment is normally in the following things

Environment

Promoting sustainable practices, waste management

Child Education

The right path of securing the future

Community Development

Infrastructure projects, healthcare access, and economic empowerment.

Education and literacy

Supporting local schools, libraries, and literacy programs.

Arts and Culture

Encouraging creative expression, cultural preservation, and community engagement.

Disaster Management

Providing aid, support, and resources during crisis situations.
